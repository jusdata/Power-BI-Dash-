## 🔹 Power BI Dashboards

### 1. Manufacturing Efficiency Dashboard

- **Tools/Skills:** Power BI, DAX & Measures, Power Query, Slicers & Filters, Bookmarks & Navigation, Tooltips
- **Description:** Analyzed manufacturing line efficiency, operator performance, machine-related errors, and downtime metrics to identify key production bottlenecks.
- **Insights:** Key KPIs track line efficiency rates, downtime minutes, and error sources — with machine handling emerging as the primary driver of production inefficiencies across lines. The dashboard supports operational decisions by isolating whether downtime is operator-driven or machine-driven, enabling targeted intervention at the right level. Halving machine-related errors directly improves both line efficiency and downtime minutes, suggesting that investment in machine maintenance and operator machine-handling training would yield the highest return on efficiency gains.

### 2. DPWH Infrastructure Projects — Investigative Dashboard

- **Tools/Skills:** Power BI, DAX & Measures, Power Query, Bookmarks & Navigation, Slicers & Filters, Tooltips
- **Description:** An investigative dashboard examining DPWH infrastructure projects from 2018 to 2025, covering project completion rates, contractor accountability, regional distribution, and budget allocation across the Philippines.
- **Insights:** Of the total infrastructure projects from 2018 to 2025, approximately 83% are recorded as complete — however, incomplete and not-yet-started projects still account for nearly PHP 180 billion, with Manila carrying the most incomplete projects at close to PHP 2 billion, warranting prioritized monitoring. Legacy Construction Corp. and Sunwest Inc. are the top contractors by total project cost at PHP 9B and PHP 10B respectively, while Quirante Construction Corp. flags a concern with 10 incomplete projects worth approximately PHP 900 million. A critical data gap emerges across regions — Central Luzon leads in total project count with Bulacan topping the provincial breakdown, yet significant contractor information is missing across multiple high-value projects, pointing to a transparency issue that demands further scrutiny.

### 3. GOT GOAT? LeBron vs Jordan — Comparative Dashboard

> 💡 *Power BI version of the GOAT debate — features multi-page navigation, dynamic tooltips, and richer cross-filtering compared to the [Excel version](https://github.com/jusdata/Excel-Excites).*

- **Tools/Skills:** Power BI, DAX & Measures, Power Query, Bookmarks & Navigation, Slicers & Filters, Tooltips
- **Description:** A multi-page comparative dashboard examining MJ and LeBron's career statistics, accomplishments, Finals records, and overall game productivity across their regular season and playoff careers.
- **Insights:** MJ averaged 30.1 PPG across his career and won 10 consecutive scoring titles, 5 MVPs, and went a perfect 6-0 in the NBA Finals with 6 Finals MVPs — while LeBron counters with superior longevity, averaging 25+ PPG for over 10 consecutive years alongside 7 APG and 7 RPG, making him the only player in history with 40K points, 10K assists, and 10K rebounds. The dashboard surfaces a compelling peak vs. longevity trade-off: MJ's dominance was concentrated in 15 years with two 3-peat championships and the most scoring titles in NBA history, while LeBron's 8 consecutive Finals appearances and record-breaking all-around statistical accumulation represent an equally compelling but differently defined greatness. The data supports both cases depending on the KPIs prioritized — making this dashboard a strong example of how framing and metric selection directly shape analytical conclusions.

### 4. Flood Control Projects Watch — Investigative Dashboard

> ⚠️ *Note: 2025 data is partial and does not represent the full year.*

- **Tools/Skills:** Power BI, DAX & Measures, Power Query, Bookmarks & Navigation, Slicers & Filters, Tooltips
- **Description:** A multi-page investigative dashboard monitoring DPWH Flood Control Projects (FCPs) from 2018 to 2025, covering budget allocation, contractor accountability, regional distribution, completion rates, and project turnaround time.
- **Insights:** Flood Control Projects command 95% of DPWH's infrastructure budget at ₱705B, with project volume surging post-2022 as the government prioritized infrastructure for economic recovery and climate resilience — yet 9,459 reported completions should be treated with caution given widespread missing actual completion dates. Sunwest Inc. (₱8.25B) and Legacy Construction Corp. (₱8.03B) lead contractor spending, with Sunwest handling 30 high-value projects (≥₱100M) at a 45% high-value portfolio concentration — but a critical red flag is that 60% of high-value projects have no specified contractors, raising serious transparency and accountability concerns. Region III leads in total FCP cost and high-value projects while posting the fastest average turnaround at 206 days, yet 78% of its high-value projects have no contractor on record — reinforcing that stronger disclosure requirements and data completeness standards are needed before completion figures can be fully trusted.
